DESCRIPTION

Fortnite is rated T for Teens, with its descriptors including violence and in-game purchases. The general audience often rates it a 7/10, with reviews stating its a "fun and creative battle royale", and "Requires skill yet revelrous".

Fortnite is a popular third-person perspective shooter professional eSport in which players work together in an open-world environment and battle others to survive and be the only ones left standing. It has in-depth mechanics like traps, movement modifying items, and diverse weapons to suit any preferred play style. This dynamic range of gameplay makes the game favorable to professional eSport teams since they can sign a range of players with different types of the same skill level.
