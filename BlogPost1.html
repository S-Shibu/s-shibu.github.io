Blog Post #1

What makes video games so enticing and entertaining?

Video games create a sense of challenge and excitement that when completed or achieved send a dopamine rush to your brain and makes you want to keep playing. This is what makes video games so addicting and what video game developers generate for as long as possible so you keep playing their game, putting time, money, and effort into it.

The Article: GameQuitters
